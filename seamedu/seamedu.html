Frequently Asked Questions

WHAT PROGRAMS DOES SEAMEDU OFFER IN MEDIA EDUCATION?

At Seamedu, we provide a wide range of programs within our Media School, dedicated to fostering talent for the media and technology industry. Our comprehensive offerings include Sound Engineering, Filmmaking, Photography, Visual Effects & Animation, Game Art & Design, Game Development, Journalism and Media Production, Media & IT, and Visual Communication. Each program at our Media School is meticulously designed to blend technological expertise with practical skills, equipping students to thrive in the rapidly evolving landscape of media and technology.

WHAT ARE THE ADMISSION REQUIREMENTS FOR SEAMEDU MEDIA SCHOOL?

Admission to Seamedu School of Pro-Exression, a prominent Media School, requires candidates to have completed 12th grade or its equivalent, with either a passing certificate or currently appearing for the final exams. Basic knowledge in the chosen field of interest, such as Sound Engineering, Filmmaking, Photography, Visual Effects & Animation, Game Art & Design, Game Development, Journalism and Media Production, Media & IT, or Visual Communication, is preferred. This ensures that students entering our Media School are equipped to engage with the curriculum and maximize their learning experience.

HOW CAN INTERNATIONAL STUDENTS APPLY FOR THE MEDIA SCHOOL PROGRAMS AT SEAMEDU?

International students interested in enrolling in programs at Seamedu's School of Pro-Expressionism, a renowned media school, can start by registering through our user-friendly Online Application Platform. It's essential to complete the Seamedu Course Admissions form accurately, ensuring all required information is provided. Additionally, applicants need to upload scanned copies of essential documents, including a valid Passport, high school transcripts, bachelor's degree transcripts (if applicable), and any previous Indian VISA copies. A non-refundable fee of Rs. 1000 for STAC (Seamedu Technical Assessment and Counseling) is also required. Once these steps are completed, a confirmation email will be sent to the registered email address, acknowledging the successful submission of the application for Seamedu Course Admissions. For further assistance or inquiries, international students are encouraged to reach out to us at student.success@seamedu.com.

WHAT KIND OF CAREER SUPPORT AND PLACEMENT SERVICES DOES SEAMEDU PROVIDE IN THE MEDIA AND ENTERTAINMENT COURSES?

At Seamedu, we are committed to providing comprehensive career support and placement services to our students in the media and entertainment courses. Our dedicated placement cell collaborates closely with industry partners to facilitate internships, job placements, and career guidance workshops. We offer personalized career counseling sessions, resume building workshops, and mock interviews to prepare students for the competitive industry landscape. Additionally, our alumni network provides valuable connections and mentorship opportunities, enhancing career prospects for graduates. We strive to ensure that every student receives the necessary support to launch a successful career in the dynamic fields of media and entertainment.

DOES SEAMEDU'S MEDIA SCHOOL OFFER ONLINE MEDIA COURSES OR DISTANCE LEARNING OPTIONS?

No, Seamedu does not currently offer online media courses or distance learning options. Our programs are designed to provide hands-on, experiential learning that integrates practical training with industry-relevant skills. We emphasize interactive classroom sessions, workshops, and industry participation to ensure students gain real-world experience essential for careers in media and entertainment. For those seeking a dynamic learning environment with direct interaction and practical application, Seamedu provides on-campus programs tailored to meet industry standards and prepare students for professional success.
